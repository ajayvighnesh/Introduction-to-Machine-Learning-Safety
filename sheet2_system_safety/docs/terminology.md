# Safety Terminology

## Loss

A loss is an adverse consequence involving harm to people, property, or legal compliance.

Example:
- Injury to pedestrians caused by autonomous driving failure.

## Hazard

A hazard is a system state that can lead to a loss under certain conditions.

Example:
- Vehicle continues driving while a pedestrian is in its path.

## Risk

Risk combines:
- probability of hazard occurrence
- probability that the hazard causes loss
- severity of the resulting loss

Approximation:

Risk ≈ P(hazard) × P(loss | hazard) × Severity
